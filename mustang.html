Mustang GT

The 2026 Ford Mustang GT is a performance-focused American muscle car featuring a 5.0L Gen-4 Coyote™ V8 engine, delivering up to 486 horsepower and 418 lb-ft of torque. It offers a 6-speed manual or 10-speed automatic transmission, with features like an active valve performance exhaust, 12.4" digital cluster with Track Apps®, and classic styling with modern updates.
